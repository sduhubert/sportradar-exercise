Project development was supported by AI.

The main use of AI technologies was optimizing the time it would take to develop the project,
given the short deadline, alongside debugging, bugfixing, and syntax-related corrections.

AI provided extensive help when it came to any issues with docker containerization, 
data inconsistencies between service interfaces, and general support with
solutions that in my mind would work, even when they didn't.

AI was also used for a considerable part of the UI design, as it was not the main goal
of the task, yet I still wanted to ensure it looked presentable and readable.
